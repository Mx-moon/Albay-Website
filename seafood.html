How to Find Seafood Restaurants

Many seafood restaurants in Albay are located near coastal roads, bay areas, ports, and city dining districts where fresh catch is regularly delivered. Legazpi City, Tabaco, and other coastal areas are good places to start if you are looking for grilled fish, shrimp, crabs, squid, and seafood platters. Before heading out, it is helpful to search the restaurant name on Google Maps, check if it is near the shoreline or main road, and confirm whether it is easy to reach by tricycle or private vehicle. You can also ask locals, hotel staff, market vendors, or drivers which seafood restaurants are known for fresh ingredients, good prices, and popular dishes.

Seafood Dining Tips

Ask the staff which seafood is freshest that day and confirm if the price is per serving, per platter, or by weight so you can manage your budget more easily. Visiting a little before the dinner rush can help you find seats faster, especially in popular restaurants near the bay or coastal areas. Bring enough cash for smaller seafood places, and do not hesitate to ask if the restaurant can adjust the spice level, cooking style, or serving size based on your preference. If you are dining with a group, ordering different seafood dishes to share is one of the best ways to enjoy more flavors in a single meal.

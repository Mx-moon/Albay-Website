Travel Tips

Book your luxury hotel early, especially during weekends, holidays, and festival seasons, because premium rooms can fill up quickly. Check the hotel’s amenities, location, and guest reviews before booking. It is also a good idea to ask if breakfast, airport transfer, or access to special facilities is already included in the price.
